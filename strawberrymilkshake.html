INCREDIENTS


8 frozen strawberries,
quartered 1 c. milk
1 pt. strawberry ice cream
Whipped Cream, for serving
Strawberries, for garnish

PROCEDURE

step 1:Wash the strawberries and pat dry them. Remove stems and cut into halves.
step 2:Add milk, ice cream and halved strawberries in a blender jar. If you like, you
can add 1 teaspoon sugar or honey for extra sweetness.
step 3:Blend until smooth puree. Pour it into serving glasses. Garnish it and serve.
